Instructions

The cards are dealt to the player and to the dealer
One of the dealers cards is flipped downv The goal of the game is to get as close to 21 withouth going over
The player has to option to get another card by clicking the "Hit Me" button
By clicking the "Stand" button the dealers card is revealed and whoever is closer to 21 without going over wins
When calculating the total the card values are calculated in the following manner:
Ace: 1 or 11
2-9: Their value
10,Jack,Queen,King: 10
If the first two cards are worth the same the player wil be given a "Split" button
Clicking that will split the players hand into two independant hands each with one of the original cards and each hand will receive one more card
